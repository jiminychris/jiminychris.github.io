         \OF ZEBULON, NORTH CAROLINA.)

         \outdoors under a tree. He fiddles with
         \an old ice box on the street. ALICE
         \enters, now age 16.)

         \(She approaches Jimmy Ray.)

=ALICE

=JIMMY RAY

=ALICE

=JIMMY RAY

=ALICE

=JIMMY RAY

=ALICE

=JIMMY RAY

=ALICE

=JIMMY RAY
